Welcome to Personify

Personify is a machine learning based model that learns from the songs that uses the songs that you like the most and provides some recommendations in return! By logging in through your Spotify account and hitting 'generate', our model gets to analyze songs it hasn't seen before, and use these songs to predict which songs from our database that you'll also like.

Personify uses an algorithm called k-means clustering to make predictions. Our database with nearly 18,000 songs also contains numerical audio analysis for each song. K-means works by splitting all the songs based on those features, grouping and re-grouping until the most generative or predictive groups are formed. We add your songs to our database and re-train our model with them added. Once the model decides which groups, or clusters, your songs fall into, we use your most common cluster to pick new recommendations from.
